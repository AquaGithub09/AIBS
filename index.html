Apartment Inventory & Billing System

AIBS is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the management of apartment inventories and billing processes. Whether you are a property manager or a tenant, AIBS provides an intuitive interface to keep track of all your apartment-related needs.

Features include real-time inventory updates, tenant-specific bailing reports, automated reminders, and support for multiple apartment units. Designed with usability and efficiency in mind, this platform streamlines property operations from a single dashboard.

Key Features

Smart Inventory

Track furniture, appliances, and fixtures in real-time, per unit or building-wide.

Automated Billing

Generate monthly bills based on utilities, maintenance, and rent with zero manual work.

Tenant Portal

Let tenants view their statements, pay online, and report issues directly.
